Crepioca homemade recipe

Homemade brazilian crepioca recipe

This is my own version of brazilian crepioca

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp of soy milk
  • 1 full tbsp of cassava strach

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients into a bowl.
  2. With the help of a fork, mix all the ingredients.
  3. Pour all the ingredients into a non-stick frying pan (previously unted with your preffered oil).
