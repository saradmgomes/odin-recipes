Homemade Bolognese Sauce

This is an homemade recipe made by myself. Follow it and you will never be mistaken again.

Ingredients for the sauce

  • 200g onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 500g of fresh tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 50ml white wine
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and the garlic
  2. Add the olive oil, the salt and the bay leaf
  3. Saute them in a large pan until the onions become golden
  4. Add the tomatoes and the white wine
  5. cook in medium heat for 5 minutes
  6. blend it until become smooth
