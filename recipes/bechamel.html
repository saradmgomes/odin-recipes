Homemade bechamel sauce for lasagna

Bechamel sauce

This is my own healthy version for bechamel sauce

Ingredients

  • 30g of corn starch
  • 500ml soy milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • pinch of black pepper
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps

  1. Boil the soy milk in a large pan
  2. Add the salt, the nutmeg the black pepper and the butter and stir.
  3. In a bowl, mix the corn starch with a little of cold water
  4. Add the corn starch slowly while stiring the soy milk.
